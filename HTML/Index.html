About Us

Since our modest beginnings in 2011, Golden Spoon has been serving delightful experiences through classic Indian food for more than a decade. A cozy, relaxing space combined with flavourful dishes makes it a first choice for every foodie in town. We welcome you to sit back, unwind and appreciate the lovely sights while our expert chefs set you up for a scrumptious feast utilizing the best and freshest ingredients. We believe that food is therapy and try to put up a smile on your face with our culinary skills. It is the best choice for anyone who wants to enjoy the best quality food at reasonable prices.
